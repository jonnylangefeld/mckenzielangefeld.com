Bio

Mckenzie Camp is a percussionist in San Francisco where she plays with many ensembles in the bay area. She is on the sub list with the San Francisco Opera, and has played with groups including Berkeley Symphony, Symphony Silicon Valley, Oakland Symphony, Santa Cruz Symphony, Chanticleer, San Francisco Contemporary Music Players, Island City Opera, Adesso Ensemble, Brass Band Mission, San Jose Chamber Orchestra, Patriot Brass, and Opera Parallele.
